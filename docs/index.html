Everything a dictation app should be.
Nothing it shouldn't.

No account. No upload. No monthly fee. Just your voice, turned into text, instantly, anywhere you can type.

🎙️

Dictate anywhere

Tap ⌘⇧Space to start and stop, or hold it like a walkie-talkie. Text is inserted into whatever app has focus — editor, browser, terminal, Slack. Esc cancels.

⚡

Real-time on the Neural Engine

Parakeet TDT v3 runs at ~110× realtime with punctuation and capitalization built in. Words appear in a live HUD as you speak.

🔒

Truly local

Audio is captured, transcribed, and inserted entirely on-device. Nothing is uploaded, logged, or phoned home. Ever.

✨

AI rewrite modes

Turn rambling speech into a clean message, a professional email, bullet notes, or a precise coding-agent prompt — via any local LLM.

🧠

Per-app & custom vocab

Auto-switch modes by frontmost app, and teach it the names and jargon you use so “Juspay” never becomes “just pay”.

📊

History & stats that stay yours

Every dictation is searchable on your Mac, with words dictated, speaking WPM, and time saved versus typing — stored in a local database, never the cloud.

Three keys to text.

From silence to inserted text in under a second.

1

Press

Hit ⌘⇧Space from any app. A floating glass HUD appears with a live waveform.

2

Speak

Talk naturally. The transcript forms in real time, on-device, as you go.

3

Done

Release. Clean, punctuated text is pasted at your cursor. Back to work.

100% on-device

Your voice is not a product.

Cloud dictation tools stream your microphone to someone else's servers. Undertone doesn't have a server. The model runs on your Neural Engine, the transcript is written by your Mac, and the history lives in a local database only you can read. It works on a plane, in airplane mode, behind the strictest firewall.

Up and running in a minute.

Apple Silicon Mac on macOS 14.4+. Xcode command-line tools to build.

zsh — undertone 
git clone https://github.com/harshpreet931/undertone.git
cd undertone
make app                                 # build + sign the .app
cp -R dist/Undertone.app /Applications   # install
open /Applications/Undertone.app         # 🎙️ menu bar

Stop typing what you could just say.
